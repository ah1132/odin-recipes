Chocolate Cake Recipe

A great chocolate cake for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • eggs
  • chocolate
  • milk
  • sugar

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into a mixing bowl and mix until combined
  2. Pour into a baking tray
  3. Place baking tray into oven and bake at 200 degrees for 25 minutes
  4. Take tray out of oven and allow to rest for 10 minutes
  5. Cut into peices and enjoy!
