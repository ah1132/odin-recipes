One Pot Chicken and Vegetables

A quick and simple beef burger recipe.

Ingredients

  • 200g beef mince
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Shape beef mince into a round patty
  2. Cook beef patty on high heat
  3. Toast burger buns
  4. Assemble remaining ingredients on burger bun
